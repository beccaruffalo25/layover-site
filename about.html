About

Hi, I’m Becca.

A twenty-something who lives for traveling.

Not cruises or all-inclusive resorts. I mean digging for hidden gems, learning public transportation without speaking the language, checking off bucket list moments, and sometimes hitting three countries in one week.

I love the research, the planning, and the logistics that turn traveling into a real trip. That started when I studied abroad in Milan, cramming weekends with Ryanair flights, risky hotel rooms, and a backpack stuffed to the brim. Since then, I’ve taken every chance I can to keep exploring and get better at doing it well.

My travel style lives somewhere between backpacking and luxury. I don’t aim for either extreme. I care about getting the best experience for the money spent while staying safe, comfortable, and unhurried.

Here I hope to share the process behind the destinations. How to plan better trips. How to maximize your budget. How to see beautiful, incredible things and leave inspired to book your next adventure.

The name comes from my favorite part of a trip — the in-between.

“I’m Becca Ruffalo, and this is LAYOVER.”
